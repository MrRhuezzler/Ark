1994 QMW Uniform Platform Tournament.  5th out of 16.

1995 WCCC. Hong Kong, 17th (tied) out of 24, with 2.5 out of 6.0.

1995 WMCCC.  Paderborn, Germany.  3rd (tied) out of 32, awarded title of World Microcomputer Amateur Chess Champion on tie-break points.

1995 WMCCC.  Paderborn, Germany, speed chess event.  There was a 10-round qualifier played against a group of humans at quick time control.  Ferret scored 9.5 points, which was good enough to tie for third (!) place.  Five programs scored 9.0 or better if I recall correctly.

1996 WMCCC.  Jakarta, Indonesia.  2nd place.

1996 WMCCC.  Jakarta, Indonesia, speed chess event.  Ferret won the event with a perfect score, earning the title of World Microcomputer Speed Chess Champion.

1997 AEGON.  Den Hague, Netherlands.  This was a human versus computer event, and Ferret performed poorly, finishing with an even score if I remember properly.

1998 WMCCC.  Paris, France.  Fifth on tie-break..

1998 WMCCC.  Paris, France, speed chess event.  Ferret won the event by a point and a half, repeating as speed chess world champion.

1999 WCCC.  Paderborn, Germany.  Tied for first place, but finished second on tie-break after drawing the playoff game as black.
